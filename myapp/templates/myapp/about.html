About This Site

Gamification is a novel and popular technique, as well as a multi-billion dollar industry. With all the news and products surrounding it, telling fact from fiction can be difficult.

This website aims to equip educators with a deeper understanding of the elements behind gamification so that they can better design courses and lesson plans in the future. Anyone is welcome, from university professors and grade-school teachers, to private tutors and even curious students.

About The Author

Eric Weng is an undergraduate Computer Science student at the UVA Engineering School. This website was created for his engineering capstone technical project, under guidance of CS Professor Mark Sherriff.
