About This Site

Gamification is a novel and popular technique, as well as a rapidly growing multi-billion dollar industry. With all the news and products surrounding it, telling fact from fiction can be difficult. At times, even researchers may buy into the hype and conduct biased or uncontrolled studies, leading to inconclusive and inaccurate results. As a result, instructors are often fed misconceptions by the media or marketing, which leads to poor student outcomes in the classroom.

This website aims to equip educators with a deeper understanding of the individual elements behind gamification using experimental results backed by published scientific studies.

Self-Hosting

This website is designed so that anyone can download and host it with minimal setup and experience. Using the data management page, you can easily create and share your custom databases. The entire source code and starter dataset is available on GitHub. Check back soon for licensing information.
