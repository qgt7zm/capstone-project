About This Site

Gamification is a popular technique, and it can be difficult to tell fact from fiction. This website aims to provide all educators a deeper understanding of the elements behind gamification so that they can better design lesson plans in the future. Anyone is welcome, from university professors and grade-school teachers to private tutors and even curious students.

About The Author

Eric Weng is an undergraduate Computer Science student at the UVA Engineering School. This website was created for his engineering capstone technical project, under guidance of CS Professor Mark Sherriff.
