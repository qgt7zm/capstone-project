What is Gamification?

Gamification is the application of game elements in non-game contexts.

Students today suffer from a lack of motivation and knowledge retention because schools prioritize memorization and standardization over individuality and critical thinking. Additionally, the recent rise of online learning and digital learning software presents additional challenges to both instructors and students. Gamification can help bring the excitement back to learning by giving students to express their creativity and problem without fear of failure.

Chances are, you have noticed the rapid spread of gamified software in the past decade, from learning software such as Kahoot! and Duolingo to even lifestyle companions such as Apple Fitness and Habitica. Perhaps you are already utilizing gamification in your classroom.

But can gamification really double your productivity? To ensure the best student performance, learning environments need to be carefully crafted to fit student needs. Start leveling up your gamification learning about the different game elements.
